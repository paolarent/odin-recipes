FISH TACOS

Description

Fish tacos are a delicious and vibrant dish that originated in coastal regions of Mexico. Typically made with flaky white fish like cod or tilapia, the fish is either grilled, fried, or battered, then placed inside warm corn or flour tortillas. They're often topped with fresh ingredients like shredded cabbage, pico de gallo, avocado, and a tangy lime crema or spicy mayo.

Details about

  • Prep Time: 40 mins
  • Cook Time: 20 mins
  • Total Time: 1 hr
  • Servings: 8

Ingredients

  • 2 lb white fish (like cod, tilapia, or mahi-mahi), cut into strips
  • 3 tablespoons lime juice (fresh)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • ½ teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 3 cups shredded cabbage (green or mixed with purple)
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 16 small corn or flour tortillas
  • ½ cup sour cream or Greek yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon hot sauce or siracha
  • 1 ripe avocado, sliced
  • Lime wedges (for serving)

Steps

  1. Step 1.
    In a bowl, mix lime juice, olive oil, garlic, cumin, paprika, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Add the fish and toss to coat. Cover and refrigerate for 30 minutes.

  2. Step 2.
    In a separate bowl, combine the shredded cabbage, cilantro, lime juice, olive oil, and a pinch of salt. Mix well and set aside.

  3. Step 3.
    In a small bowl, mix sour cream (or yogurt), mayonnaise, lime juice, hot sauce, and a pinch of salt. Stir until smooth.

  4. Step 4.
    Heat a pan over medium heat. Add a bit of oil, then cook the fish strips for about 3–4 minutes per side, until fully cooked and flaky.

  5. Step 5.
    Heat the tortillas in a dry pan or on a low flame until soft and warm. Keep them covered in a clean towel.

  6. Step 6.
    Place fish in each tortilla. Add cabbage slaw, drizzle with sauce, and top with avocado slices. Garnish with extra cilantro and serve with lime wedges.
